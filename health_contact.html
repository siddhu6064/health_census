How do I track my health condition?

You can register as a patient on our home page and select your condition from the available options. Our system will track and analyze your data over time.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All patient information is encrypted and stored securely following healthcare industry standards.

Can I export my health records?

Yes, you can export your health records in CSV format from the dashboard. Click the Export button in the Recent Patient Records section.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call our support hotline.
